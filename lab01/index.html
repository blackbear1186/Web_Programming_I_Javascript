The Top 10 Dishes of Japan

This website is for people who visit Japan or foreigners who live in Japan and want to know what is good to eat and drink. I have personally tried all of the dishes have made a list. Foreigners who live or have visited Japan may not beware that some of the dishes exist. Therefore, I wanted to present this list of the best foods so that foreigners can try these delicious dishes. I have also included links to other websites that shows highly recommended drinks, snacks, as well as food. I included snacks and food from Hokkaido on the list because Hokkaido has the best snacks in Japan and some of the best tasting dishes. If you plan on visiting Japan, I highly recommend you try the food on this list. I can almost guarantee that you will not be disappointed.
